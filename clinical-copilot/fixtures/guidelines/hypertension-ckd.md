# Hypertension in chronic kidney disease — demo guideline

## Blood pressure targets

For adults with CKD and diabetes, target blood pressure below 130/80 mmHg when tolerated. Use home blood pressure logs when clinic readings are unavailable.

## Lab frequency

Check basic metabolic panel including creatinine and potassium every 3–6 months for stable CKD stage 3, or sooner after medication changes affecting renal function.

## When to escalate nephrology referral

Refer to nephrology when eGFR is persistently below 30 mL/min/1.73m², creatinine rises more than 0.3 mg/dL within 48 hours, or proteinuria increases despite maximal tolerated ACE/ARB therapy.
